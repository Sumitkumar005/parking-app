## üè¢ **Commercial Buildings & Office Complexes**

### Scenario
A 20-story office building with 200 parking spots serving 500+ employees daily.

### Implementation
- **Employee Portal**: Staff log in each morning to book available spots
- **Tiered Pricing**: Premium spots near entrances cost more than distant ones
- **Real-time Availability**: Live updates prevent employees from searching for occupied spots
- **Automated Billing**: Monthly parking fees processed automatically through payroll

### Business Benefits
- **Reduced Congestion**: Eliminates circling for parking spots
- **Fair Allocation**: First-come-first-served system ensures equity
- **Revenue Tracking**: Detailed reports for facility management
- **Employee Satisfaction**: Guaranteed parking reduces workplace stress
